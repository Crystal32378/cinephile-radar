# Community Feedback

## Current Signal

Known community feedback so far:

> 好期待早期電影的中譯片名可以好好查證。

## Interpretation

The clearest user value is trustworthy Traditional Chinese title handling, especially for early films. This points to a careful verification workflow, not an immediate shared editing system.

## Recommended Next Step

Create an early-film title verification list with:

- festival or award
- year
- original / English title
- current Chinese title
- possible alternate Traditional Chinese titles
- source links
- confidence level
- review status
- notes

## What Not To Build Yet

- Do not build accounts.
- Do not build a shared editor.
- Do not add backend infrastructure.
- Do not invite broad community editing before the review workflow and title policy are clear.
